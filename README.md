# 🌱 **Carbon Zero**

### 💡 Tagline  
**Personalized AI tips to reduce your carbon footprint.**

---

### ❓ What it does  
**Carbon Assistant** is an AI-powered tool that analyzes your daily activities—transportation, energy use, diet—and gives personalized, actionable tips to reduce carbon emissions. It compares your footprint to global averages and provides simple suggestions for a more sustainable lifestyle.

---

### 🛠️ How we built it  
- **Backend**: Node.js + Express  
- **AI Integration**: OpenAI prompt engineering for personalized advice  
- **Data**: JSON-based user activity summary  
- **Reports**: Markdown-generated structured reports

---

### 🌍 Why it matters  
Climate change is a growing concern. **Carbon Assistant** makes it easy to take eco-friendly actions with personalized, positive, and practical suggestions for reducing your carbon footprint.

---

### 🚀 What's next  
- Turn it into a full web/mobile app  
- Integrate real-time data from smart devices
